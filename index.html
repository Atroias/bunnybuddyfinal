welcome

Founded in 2005, our restaurant was born out of a deep passion for authentic Indian cuisine and a dream to share the rich, diverse flavors of India with the world. Our founder, Pregasin, hails from a small village in India where culinary traditions have been passed down through generations. Inspired by the aromatic spices, vibrant colors, and the communal joy of Indian cooking, Pregasin embarked on a journey to bring these cherished flavors to our community.
